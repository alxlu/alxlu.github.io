#Hyperion.ai

Hyperion.ai is the product of a startup I am currently the founding CEO of. It is a custom multivitamin that improves itself over time by using artificial intelligence to continuously monitor and analyze a user's health data. This writeup will be focused primarily on the business, tech aspects, and other behind the scenes aspects of Hyperion.ai. For more information about the product itself, check out: http://hyperion.ai
